Indian weddings are renowned for their grandeur, cultural diversity, and elaborate rituals, making them one of the most vibrant and celebrated events in the world. These weddings are not just union ceremonies but rather week-long festivals filled with rituals, colors, music, and an abundance of traditions. Let's delve into the rich tapestry of Indian weddings. The heart of any Indian wedding is the sacred ceremony where the couple takes their vows. In a Hindu wedding, this is called the 'Phere,' Bright and vibrant hues dominate the décor, clothing, and even the food. Traditional wedding colors such as red, gold, and saffron are often used, symbolizing love, prosperity, and purity.
